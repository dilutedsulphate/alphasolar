# Alpha Solar Power - Website

A modern, responsive website for Alpha Solar Power, Kashmir's trusted solar energy company.

## Features

- **Hero Section**: Enhanced animated hero with particle effects, solar rays, animated counters, and CTAs
- **Services Section**: Hotel, Commercial, and Domestic solar solutions with dedicated pages
- **Accessories Page**: Solar cables, MC4 connectors, mounting rails, junction boxes, and more
- **Brands Carousel**: Animated infinite scroll of partner brands
- **About Section**: Company story, mission, values, and team
- **Testimonials**: Customer reviews with ratings
- **Blog Section**: SEO-optimized solar energy articles for Kashmir market
- **Contact Page**: Dedicated contact page with form, map, and contact info
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct WhatsApp contact button
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first, works on all devices

## Tech Stack

- React + TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui components
- React Router for navigation
- Plus Jakarta Sans & Manrope fonts
- Lucide React icons

## Color Scheme

- **Primary (Blue)**: #0066CC - Trust, Technology, Sky
- **Accent (Green)**: #22C55E - Nature, Sustainability
- **Energy (Yellow)**: #F59E0B - Sun, Energy, Warmth

## Pages

- `/` - Home page with all sections
- `/about` - Detailed about page with timeline and team
- `/blog` - All blog articles (SEO-optimized for Kashmir solar market)
- `/blog/:slug` - Individual blog post
- `/accessories` - Solar accessories and components
- `/contact` - Dedicated contact page with form, phone, email, and map
- `/services/hotels` - Hotel solar solutions (dedicated page)
- `/services/commercial` - Commercial solar solutions (dedicated page)
- `/services/domestic` - Domestic solar solutions (dedicated page)

## Navigation Order

Home > Services > Blog > Accessories > About Us > Contact
